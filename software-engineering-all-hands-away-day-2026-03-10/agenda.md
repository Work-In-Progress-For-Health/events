# Software engineering all hands away day agenda

09:00 Social time - optional (20m)

09:30 Morning opening remarks: introduce & inspire (20m)

09:50 Session 1: Where are we going? (30m)

- Can you imagine the future in exactly four years?
- Can you imagine north stars for clinicians and patients?
- Can you imagine entire population dynamics?
- Top fears: dementia, cardiac collapse, cancer
- Top waves: involvement, empowerment, encouragement

10:20 Session 2: Where are our maps and missions? (30m)

- How do you aim for goals and guides?
- How do you say yes or no or why or when?
- How do you create protected time for help?
- Top fears: the cart, the whip, the fog
- Top waves: TOM, OKR, KPI

10:50 Break (20m)

11:10 Session 3: How do we get there in Cymraeg? (30m)

- Deuparth gwaith yw ei ddechrau - Starting the work is two thirds of it
- Tyfid maban, ni thyf ei gadachan - The child will grow, his clothes will not
- Nerth gwlad, ei gwybodaeth - The strength of a nation is its knowledge
- Top fears: blame, leaving the middle of the road, weakness
- Top waves: high-trust, shift-left, all-hands

11:40 Session 4: What are our highest multipliers? (30m)

- Solutions over silos
- Transparency over secrecy
- Wellness over illness
- Top fears: NIH, exposure, confusion
- Top waves: solidarity, vulnerability, tranquility

12:10 Morning closing remarks: review & reflect (20m)

12:30 Lunch break (50m)

13:30 Afternoon opening remarks: make it real (20m)

13:50 Session 5: Moonshot Mission

- Solve a massive problem by leveraging science, technology, innovation
- High-sight high-risk multi-year trailblazer that furthers high-line OKRs
- Dreic hylaw | Skillful dragon (empowered professional NOT committees)
- Top fears: danger to patients, resistance to innovation, scarcity of resources
- Top waves: covision (we see it), coaction (we build it), coalescing (we live it)

14:20 Session 6: Lunar Lander

- Solve one small independent piece of the puzzle that is boots-on-ground right now
- Do double-diamond: 2-week three-amigos then 2-month pizza-team.
- Dreic hylaw | Skillful dragon (empowered professional leader)
- Top fears: are we aligned enough?, are we agile enough?, are we cloud enough?
- Top waves: agile, APIs, QAs

14:50 Break (20m)

15:10 Session 7: Ground Control (30m)

- How do we measure our metrics, including work progress and outcomes?
- This is a cross-cutting concern a.k.a. aspect-oriented programming a.k.a. NFR
- Coordination is our weakest link IMHO so we all must work to better it
- Top fears: one size doesn't fit all, lack of demo areas, conformance compliance
- Top waves: baseline open-source success, rainbow deployments, product-service ITIL 5

15:40 Session 8: Where do we go from here? (30m)

- Software engineering is a profession and we must step up to this
- SWE Body Of Knowledge (BOK) & SWE Community Of Practice (COP) pairing
- Continuous Professional Development (CPD) and what you want for training in FY2026

- Top fears: overwhelm, discomfort, same as it ever was
- Top waves: involvement, empowerment, encouragement (a.k.a. session 1 coda)

16:10 Afternoon closing remarks: review & reflect (20m)

16:30 Social time

17:00 Complete
