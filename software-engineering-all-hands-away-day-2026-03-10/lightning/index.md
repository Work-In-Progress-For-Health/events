# 5-minute lightning talks

On March 10, the 5-minute lighting talks will inpsire our software engineers and energize our new software engineering (SWE) community of practice (COP).

[Click here for the complete schedule with all speakers, lightning talks, etc.](..)

If you're reading this, and you're volunteering to present, then thank you so much. You're listed on the complete schedule above. Any questions or corrections? Please message me, joel.henderson@wales.nhs.uk.

This page has some quick easy guidance to help you with what you're presenting .

Use this guidance as you wish.

If you want it to be quick and easy...

- Slide 1 is about you:
  - Your name, title, email address
  - One sentence that explains your best objective for the upcoming fiscal year.
  - The purpose is anchoring us all to what all the speakers are doing with FY2026 objectives.

- Slide 2 is about the group:
  - One intriguing important relevant question (or idea, or offering, etc.) that you have for the software engineering community of practice.
  - A specific date & time you're offering for the group to learn more, such as a 1-hour workshop, or show-and-tell, or town hall, etc.
  - The purpose of this is building our bridges, seeding our discussions and participations during the day and into the spring and summer.

Please keep it to 5 minutes max, because the next speaker will be up right after you.

Your slides can look like anything you want; your slides don't need to look like corporate templates.

If you're planning a workshop or show-and-tell, then the ideal for most teams is any Tuesday, 1-hour, in-person in Cardiff TGA and also online on Teams.

Imagine a movie trailer that aims to intrigue people to learn more, and especially to come to the movie in the theatre.
