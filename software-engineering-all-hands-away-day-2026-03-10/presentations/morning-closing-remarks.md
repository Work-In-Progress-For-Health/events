# Morning Closing Remarks

20 minutes. After sessions 1, 2, 3, 4.

---

### Slide 1: What We Explored This Morning

Four sessions. Four big questions.

1. **Where are we going?** — We imagined the future in four years. We pictured north stars for clinicians and patients. We confronted fears of dementia, cardiac collapse, and cancer — and we named our levers: involvement, empowerment, encouragement.

2. **Where are our maps and missions?** — We asked how we say yes, no, why, and when. We named the cart, the whip, and the fog. We reached for TOM, OKRs, and KPIs as our navigational instruments.

3. **How do we get there in Cymraeg?** — We drew strength from Welsh proverbs. Deuparth gwaith yw ei ddechrau. We named blame, leaving the middle of the road, and weakness as fears — and answered with high-trust, shift-left, and all-hands.

4. **What are our highest multipliers?** — Solutions over silos. Transparency over secrecy. Wellness over illness. We chose solidarity, vulnerability, and tranquility.

---

### Slide 2: Patterns and Threads

Notice what keeps surfacing:

- **Trust** appears everywhere — psychological safety, blameless reflection, open discussion.
- **Clarity** appears everywhere — TOM, OKRs, KPIs, saying yes or no or why or when.
- **Togetherness** appears everywhere — all-hands, solidarity, mutual purpose, across silos.

These are not three separate things. They are one thing seen from three angles. Trust enables clarity. Clarity enables togetherness. Togetherness deepens trust.
