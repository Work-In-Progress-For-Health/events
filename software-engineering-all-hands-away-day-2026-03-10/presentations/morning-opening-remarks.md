# Morning Opening Remarks

20 minutes. Before sessions 1, 2, 3, 4.

---

### Slide 1: Croeso — Welcome to Our Away Day

Welcome to the Software Engineering All Hands Away Day.

Today we step away from our desks, our tickets, our pipelines — and we look up.

We are here because software engineering in NHS Wales matters. What we build touches clinicians and patients across the whole of Cymru. That is a privilege and a responsibility.

Today is about direction, not distraction. We are here to think together, talk together, and decide together.

---

### Slide 2: The Shape of the Day

**Morning (Sessions 1–4)**

1. Where are we going?
2. Where are our maps and missions?
3. How do we get there in Cymraeg?
4. What are our highest multipliers?

**Afternoon (Sessions 5–8)**

5. Moonshot Mission
6. Lunar Lander
7. Ground Control
8. Where do we go from here?

Each session is 30 minutes: a 15-minute talk followed by a 15-minute workshop. Your voice matters in every workshop.

---

### Slide 3: How We Work Today

Every session surfaces three fears and three levers. Fears are honest. Levers are hopeful. Both are necessary.

The workshops are not theatre. They are working sessions. Bring your experience, your doubts, your ideas. Write on the walls. Challenge each other with kindness.

Ground rules:

- Listen more than you speak
- Assume good intent
- Stay curious, not certain

---

### Slide 4: Why This Day Matters

We are not here to produce a document. We are here to produce alignment.

By lunchtime you will have explored where we are going, how we navigate, how we draw on our Welsh identity, and what multiplies our impact.

The morning ends with three words that will echo all day:

- **Involvement** — everyone contributes
- **Empowerment** — everyone decides
- **Encouragement** — everyone supports

Let's begin.
