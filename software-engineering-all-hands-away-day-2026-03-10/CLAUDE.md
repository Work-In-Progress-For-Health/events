# Software engineering all hands away day

## Session 1: Where are we going? (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- Can you imagine the future in exactly four years?
- Can you imagine north stars for clinicians and patients?
- Can you imagine entire population dynamics?

Top fears:

- dementia
- cardiac collapse
- cancer

Top levers:

- involvement
- empowerment
- encouragement

## Session 2: Where are our maps and missions? (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- How do you aim for goals and guides?
- How do you say yes or no or why or when?
- How do you create protected time for help?

Top fears:

- the cart (a.k.a. putting the cart before the horse)
- the whip (a.k.a. crack-the-whip, whiplash, too much failure mode)
- the fog (a.k.a. the fog of war, flying in fog without orientation)

Top levers:

- Target Operating Model (TOM)
- Objectives & Key Results (OKRs)
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Session 3: How do we get there in Cymraeg? (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- Deuparth gwaith yw ei ddechrau - Starting the work is two thirds of it
- Tyfid maban, ni thyf ei gadachan - The child will grow, his clothes will not
- Nerth gwlad, ei gwybodaeth - The strength of a nation is its knowledge

Top fears:

- blame
- leaving the middle of the road
- weakness

Top levers:

- high-trust (a.k.a. pychological safety, blameless reflection, make mistakes faster)
- shift-left (a.k.a. agile discovery, rapid prototyping, behavior driven development)
- all-hands (a.k.a. we all work together, across silos, mutual purpose)

## Session 4: What are our highest multipliers? (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- Solutions over silos
- Transparency over secrecy
- Wellness over illness

Top fears:

- not invented here (NIH)
- exposure (of secrets, of confidential information, of work in progress)
- confusion (unclear how to prioritize wellness over illess, or agile over waterfall)

Top levers:

- solidarity (a.k.a. we all support each other)
- vulnerability (a.k.a. open discussion, public sharing, transparent ways of working)
- tranquility (a.k.a. calm and steady and sustainable ways of working)

## Session 5: Moonshot Mission (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- Solve a massive problem by leveraging science, technology, innovation
- High-sight high-risk multi-year trailblazer that furthers high-line TOM and OKRs
- Dreic hylaw | Skillful dragon (empowered professional NOT committees)

Top fears:

- danger to patients
- resistance to innovation
- scarcity of resources

Top levers:

- covision (we see it)
- coaction (we build it)
- coalescing (we live it)

## Session 6: Lunar Lander (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- Solve one small independent piece of the puzzle that is boots-on-ground right now
- Do double-diamond process: 2-week three-amigos for discovery + design, then 2-month pizza-team for development + delivery.
- Dreic hylaw | Skillful dragon (a.k.a. empowered professional leader)

Top fears:

- are we aligned enough?
- are we agile enough?
- are we cloud enough?

Top levers:

- signing from the same hymnbooks
- agile principles
- cloud sandboxes

## Session 7: Ground Control (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- How do we measure our metrics, including work progress and outcomes?
- This is a cross-cutting concern a.k.a. aspect-oriented programming a.k.a. NFR
- Coordination is our weakest link IMHO so we all must work to better it

Top fears:

- one size doesn't fit all
- lack of demo areas
- conformance compliance

Top levers:

- baseline open-source success
- rainbow deployments (a.k.a. each topic gets its own deployment)
- product-service ITIL 5

## Session 8: Where do we go from here? (15 minute talk, 15 minute workshop)

3 slides:

- Software engineering is a profession and we must step up to this
- SWE Body Of Knowledge (BOK) & SWE Community Of Practice (COP) pairing
- Continuous Professional Development (CPD) and what you want for training in FY2026

Top fears:

- overwhelm
- discomfort
- same as it ever was

Top levers: (redux of session 1 top levers)

- involvement
- empowerment
- encouragement
